The Automated Kalamay Machine modernizes traditional kalamay production by integrating automated cooking, continuous stirring, and smart control systems. It improves efficiency, consistency, safety, and hygiene for small- to medium-scale production.

Key Features

Automated Stirring

Continuous mixing prevents burning and ensures uniform cooking.

Touchscreen Control

User-friendly interface for monitoring and controlling the process.

Safety Breaker

Built-in circuit breaker protects the system from electrical faults.

Stainless Housing

Food-grade stainless steel ensures durability and hygiene.

Machine System Overview

Cooking & Mixing Machine

The main cooking unit contains the heating system, mixing pot, stirrer, touchscreen panel, and breaker. It performs the cooking and mixing of kalamay automatically.

Coconut Grater & Shredder

A separate but connected machine processes fresh coconuts. The grated coconut is transferred to the cooking machine through a chute or conveyor.

Safety & Hygiene

The Automated Kalamay Machine is designed with safety switches, electrical protection, insulated housing, and easy-to-clean parts to ensure safe and hygienic operation.
